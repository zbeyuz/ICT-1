FAQ

What type of payment do you accept?
For local orders, we accept cash on delivery as our primary mode of payment. We also accept Paypal payment via Paypal Express Checkout - This is also for overseas payment.
What are the types of delivery services & self collection that you provide?
For queries on this, do check out our delivery guide or email us at sales@beststyleforever.com
What are your return policies?

If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it as long as it fulfils the following conditions:

- Item is returned within 7 working days from item receipt by mail.

- Item is returned within 14 working days from item receipt in the concept store.

- Item is in original condition, unwashed, and with the packaging bag. Accessories are not applicable for return due to hygiene purposes.

How do I make a return?
Drop us a mail at sales@beststyleforever.com where we will process the return and issue you with the return address. Depending on the case/situation, we may provide a one-to-one exchange, exchange with another item of your choice or an order credit.
Are your items available offline?
Yes! We are available at our concept shop location at 571 Sunrise Avenue - Singapore. Opening hours Monday - Friday: 8.00 - 20.00. Saturday: 9.00 - 15.00. Sunday: Closed
